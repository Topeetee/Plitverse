Company Introduction – Plitverse Solutions Ltd

Plitverse Solutions is a registered Private Company (Registration No. 8856961). The business has been in operation for over five years under the registered business name Litresumesplus, before transitioning in 2025 into a fully incorporated private company under its new identity.

This transition reflects not only our growth and expansion, but also our commitment to professionalism, credibility, and long-term brand sustainability.

Built on the pillars of People, Leadership, Innovation, and Technology (PLIT), Plitverse Solutions partners with organizations to enhance efficiency, develop talent, and drive sustainable growth through tailored consulting and technology-driven solutions.
